# What's new in Blurt

_Changes since v0.1.29._

> **Heads up:** Blurt now requires **macOS 26 (Tahoe)** or later.

- **A fresh new look.** Blurt adopts macOS Tahoe's Liquid Glass design — the
  recording pill and main window now use the system's native glass styling.

- **See your recent blurts.** The main window now shows your last three
  dictations with timestamps. Hover over one to read the full text.

- **Copy any recent blurt.** Hover over a recent dictation (or right-click it)
  and click **Copy** to put it back on your clipboard.

- **More private by default.** Blurt no longer writes your dictations to a log
  file unless you explicitly turn on the new **Developer Mode** switch in
  Settings. The Prompt Inspector window has been removed.

- **Faster and smoother.** Recording starts more quickly when you press your
  shortcut, and the recording pill animates more efficiently.

- **Visual polish.** Fixed a hard line that could appear around the recording
  pill's shadow, and the "Copied" confirmation now shows as a simple, quiet
  status message.
